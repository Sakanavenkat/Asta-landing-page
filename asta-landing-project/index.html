The flow

Three steps, one conversation

Every exchange with ASTA follows the same real-time loop — this is the actual order it happens in, not just a design device.

01

You speak

Your voice is captured in the browser and streamed to ASTA's backend — no typing required.

02

ASTA thinks

Speech is transcribed and passed to Groq's Llama 3.3 70B for a fast, context-aware response.

03

ASTA replies

The response is converted back to speech and played to you — a full voice round-trip.
